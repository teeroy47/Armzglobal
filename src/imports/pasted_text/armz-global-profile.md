1. MANNED SECURITY GUARDS
Armz Global delivers superior guarding services. We value long term relationships
with clients and develops a long understanding that results in quality security outcomes.
When providing guarding services, we take time to understand your needs and your
environment as well as full risk analysis to ensure that our security officers will fit your
specific requirements. Our security guards are well equipped with a fully geared uniform,
button sticks and handcuffs and a panic button to communicate with our control room.
SUPERVISION
To ensure that our guards complete their duties accordingly, and in accordance with
company policy, Armz Global has Supervisors whose sole task is to check on the
guards once during the day and twice at night. Also, we have a guard tour system
that we use to help manage full coverage of the area by the guards whereby they will
need to scan on the pre-set areas using the gadgets provided by the company which
even the client will have access to that system so that they can see if they are being
protected as per the agreement and requirement.
